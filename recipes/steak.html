Steak Recipe

Description

Follow this recipe to cook a delicious, juicy steak!

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • 1 steak of your choosing

Steps

  1. Remove packaging and pat meat dry with paper towels. Set to dry for 30-60 mins.
  2. Put skillet on the stove and sprinkle light salt. Turn to high heat.
  3. When pan is smoking hot, 5 to 8 minutes, pat steak dry again and place in pan.
  4. Let steak sizzle for 1 min. Then flip steak every 30 seconds. Sprinkle some salt.
  5. When steak has contracted in size and developed a dark-brown crust, about 4 minutes total, check for doneness.
  6. Remove steak to a cutting board and tent lightly with foil. Let rest 5 minutes, then serve!
